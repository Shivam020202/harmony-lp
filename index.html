How Deep TMS Therapy Works

Advanced, FDA-cleared treatment using targeted magnetic pulses to stimulate neural circuits

What is Deep TMS?

Deep Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (Deep TMS) is an advanced, non-invasive procedure that uses targeted magnetic pulses to stimulate neural circuits deeper within the brain. It's FDA-cleared for certain treatment-resistant conditions and has helped thousands of patients find relief.

Unlike medications, Deep TMS targets specific brain regions involved in mood and behavior, offering a precision approach with minimal systemic side effects.

1

Non-Invasive

No surgery, no anesthesia, no systemic side effects. Treatment is performed in our comfortable clinic.

2

Proven Effective

FDA-approved with clinical studies showing 80% response rates and 65% remission rates in treatment-resistant individuals.

3

Minimal Side Effects

Most patients experience no significant side effects, unlike many psychiatric medications.
